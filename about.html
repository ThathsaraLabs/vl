About Our Company

Vector Labs AI is an elite engineering enterprise designing production-grade artificial intelligence workflows and custom platforms from Gimhae-si, South Korea.

Our History & Identity

Founded on **May 20, 2026**, Vector Labs AI was launched to address the gap between raw computational capability and reliable enterprise software. We recognized that while deep learning structures were advancing rapidly, companies struggled to deploy them in high-concurrency environments safely.

Headquartered in the technological hub of Gyeongsangnam-do, Republic of Korea, we operate at the intersection of embedded systems design, neural networks, and high-performance server architectures.

Today, our multi-disciplinary engineering squads design, deploy, and maintain robust systems for global logistics, data analytics providers, and automated digital media workflows.
